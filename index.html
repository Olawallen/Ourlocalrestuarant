Nigerian meal trivia

What is the most consumed Nigerian meal?

Jollof rice is the most commonly eaten Nigerian meal. It is a rice dish cooked in a tomato-based sauce, and it is typically served with chicken, beef, or fish. Jollof rice is a national dish of Nigeria, and it is enjoyed by people of all ages and backgrounds. This is the most popular Nigeria food in Nigeria and also reputed as the national dish of Nigeria. You will find jollof rice in any popular restaurant or eatery in Nigeria. Most Nigerian home serve jollof rice at least twice in a week. Source: allnigerianfoods
